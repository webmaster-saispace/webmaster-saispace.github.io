mirrordome

In March 2011, a mirrordome system was commissioned at the Planetarium, enabling Full-Dome shows with video covering the entire dome. The method conceived and popularized by Paul Bourke was used, with his help.

This picture below of the image projected on the dome shows the old star projector in the foreground.


The fulldome shows available at the Sri Sathya Sai Space Theatre are listed at our shows page.
